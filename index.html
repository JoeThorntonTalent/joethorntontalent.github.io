About me

I joined Playfair Capital in 2015 to help build technology companies that are disrupting the way we live, work and play for the better. In particular, I'm responsible for finding and hiring exceptional people for our fast growing startups. You can read more about Playfair's focus here and check out our existing porfolio here.

As Head of Talent at Playfair, I help our founders to build high perfomance teams and implement scalable talent solutions. I teach founders how to aggressively pursue the best talent in the World and educate them on the effort required to succesfullly build a startup team. As a proactive and hands-on recruiter, I spend much of my time making critical hires for our startups.

Prior to Playfair, I was a Technical Recruiter at Facebook and Google and earned two law degrees from the National University of Ireland, Galway. In my spare time I play American Football, improve my chess game, drink coffee and explore London. I'm also a qualified skydiver in constant pursuit of blue skies.
