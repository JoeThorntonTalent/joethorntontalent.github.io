About me

I joined Playfair Capital in 2013 and focus on consumer and enterprise technology-enabled investments, specifically deal sourcing, due diligence, and ongoing portfolio support. I'm particularly interested in artificial intelligence and machine learning, infrastructure-as-a-service, mobile-first on-demand consumer apps, enterprise productivity software, as well as genomics. Within the portfolio, I focus on product design, analytics, and growth, while providing support for companies raising their next round of financing.

At Playfair, I've led, originated or participated in seed, Series A and Series B investments for high-growth companies including Mapillary, Appear Here, Dojo, and Festicket. Prior to Playfair, I earned an M.Phil and Ph.D in oncology as a Gates and Dr. Herchel Smith Scholar at the University of Cambridge, and a BA in biology from Williams College. I've published research focused on technologies to halt the fatal spread of cancer around the body.
